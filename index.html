Handcrafted sound panels because your sound is important

Nashville Sound Panels started with a simple desire: make Nashville sound better. Over seven years we treated more than fifty spaces, sponsored recording sessions for local musicians, and came to know countless producers, engineers, and business owners who make Music City what it is.

NSP's operations ceased in February of 2022. The products, the rooms, and the relationships built inside this city live on.

The spaces of Music City should embody the full spectrum of sounds that originate here and are heard around the world. This page is a small archive of work — what we built, where it lived, and the people who made it possible.

— Joe Pillatsch Founder, Nashville Sound Panels

— 04 Selected Work

Home Studios

Producers, engineers, and players carved space out of bungalows and back rooms. We tuned what they built — wood-framed broadband panels, custom prints, room-by-room measurement.

— 05Selected Work

Commercial Spaces

Offices, taprooms, and creative studios — places people work and gather. Acoustic treatment that disappears into the architecture and lets the room do its job. 6 frames·Marathon Village · Donelson

— 06Selected Work

Residential Spaces

Living rooms, listening rooms, and the corners of homes where music actually happens. Treatment chosen to live alongside furniture, art, and life. 5 spaces·Across Nashville

Places of Worship

Sanctuaries built for music, voice, and gathering — the most acoustically demanding rooms we touched. Treatment placed with reverence for stained glass, brick, and decades of tradition. 3 spaces·TN · IL
