Nashville Sound Panels

Our business started with a simple desire to “make Nashville sound better”. As Music City transformed from the country capital of America to home of every genre imaginable, our goal was to bring function and style to spaces where music was enjoyed and created.

Over 7 years we treated 50+ spaces, sponsored recording sessions for local musicians and came to know countless local producers, musicians and business owners.

While NSP's operations ceased in February 2022, our products and relationships within the city live on.

Rooms should sound better - but they should also reflect the personalities of the people within them. And Music City should reflect the dynamism of the sounds made here that are heard around the world.

Sincerely,
Joe Pillatsch, Founder of Nashville Sound Panels
